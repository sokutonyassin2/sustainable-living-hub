The Visionary

Who is BOS?

William Odinga Balikuddembe is the foundational spirit behind the Bill of Senga. He represents a profound synthesis of ancestral wisdom and progressive agricultural science.

We believe that true sustainability isn't just about preserving what we have; it's about actively regenerating the earth. By returning to indigenous practices validated by modern science, we cultivate environments where life thrives at every level.
