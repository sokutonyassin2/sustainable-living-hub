Our Philosophical Horizon

Bill of Senga (BOS) represents a unique synthesis of traditional wisdom and modern ecological science. Founded in Kyampisi, Mukono, Uganda, our pilot demonstration hub was established on depleted, dry land. Over nearly two decades, we've unlocked the regenerative power of organic farming, cover cropping, smart water systems, and selective forest restoration.

Today, BOS is a thriving research institute, community practical school, and champion agroecology farm. We believe that feeding humanity does not require the destruction of our ecosystems. Through scientific research, grassroots outreach, and our innovative "Tree Billionaire" youth engagement, we are showing how modern food sovereignty and nature can grow together.
